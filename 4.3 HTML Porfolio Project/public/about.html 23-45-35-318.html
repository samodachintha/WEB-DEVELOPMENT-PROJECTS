About Me

Hi, I’m Pannilage Samod Achintha — an operations and administration professional with hands-on experience in logistics, documentation, and business coordination within the aquatics and import/export industry.

I began my career at Lanka Pets Aquatics in 2020 as a Documentation Assistant and steadily grew into leadership roles, including Operations Manager and later Administration Manager. Over the years, I’ve developed strong expertise in import/export documentation, shipment coordination, financial handling for international transactions, and regulatory compliance.

My work involves close collaboration with airport cargo teams and government authorities such as Sri Lanka Customs, Inland Revenue Department, Department of Aquatic Resources, and NAQDA to ensure smooth and compliant operations.

I’m passionate about process improvement, operational efficiency, and building reliable systems that support business growth. I enjoy learning new technologies, exploring digital solutions, and working on projects that combine business operations with tech innovation.

Outside of work, I’m a traveler and planner who enjoys organizing international trips and discovering new experiences across Asia.
